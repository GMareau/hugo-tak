The Agile Kaleidoscope

Fabrice

Shooted at Agile Tour Aix-Marseille 2018

Fabrice is a lead tech.

He’s a former colleague and a friend.

His experience with the Liberating Structure Troika Consulting reminded him that the simplest solutions are often the better and that it is easier to step back when you’re not the one impacted by a problem.

The sense of urgency mentioned by Leo in his keynote also resonated strongly with him.
